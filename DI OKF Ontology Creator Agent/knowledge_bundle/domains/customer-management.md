# Customer Management

## Description
Customer master and segmentation domain used for customer-centric bookings analysis.

## Business Purpose
This domain provides the customer identity and segmentation context needed to analyze bookings by account, segment, industry, and headquarters location. In the quote-to-booking process, customer segment and coverage model are confirmed during opportunity qualification.
