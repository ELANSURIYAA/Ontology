# Product Management

## Description
Product and offer domain used for portfolio, family, and technology analysis.

This domain supports portfolio and product-mix analysis across Cisco offers. The business process states that products are configured in Cisco Commerce Workspace, priced, discounted, and ultimately associated to booking recognition.
