# Contract Management

## Description
Contract descriptor domain used for term, coverage, and renewal-related analysis.

## Business Purpose
This domain supports analysis of service, support, and subscription agreement characteristics associated with bookings. The business process confirms that contracts and entitlements are created after booking and feed the renewal lifecycle.
